# Kokoro Five-Level Curriculum

> Private curriculum source note for E50. Use for course reconstruction and
> router/skill upgrade planning.

## Canonical Levels

1. **Raíz: Diagnóstico y Fundamentos**
   - Diagnóstico, fallas invisibles, Montaña del Mañana, funnel desde empatía.
   - Client outcome: sentirse visto y recibir ofertas éticas y educativas.

2. **Semilla: Modelo y Producto**
   - El modelo de negocio es el producto.
   - Lean Canvas, Whole Product, Customer Forces Model, validación temprana.
   - Client outcome: productos coherentes, deseables y viables.

3. **Brote: Validación y Campañas**
   - Metodología PESCAR.
   - Validation Plan & Experiment Report.
   - Campaigns as conscious experiments with metrics, documentation, and
     learning loops.

4. **Ramas: Meta Ads y Storytelling**
   - Introducción a Meta Ads.
   - Storytelling "Hamburguesa" / AAIDA.
   - Meta as a cyclical flywheel and emotional communication system.

5. **Fruto: Kaizen y Mejora Continua**
   - Continuous improvement system.
   - Process optimization, market listening, model evolution, sustainable
     profitable growth.

## Correction To Preserve

The formal course curriculum uses five levels. Existing Kokoro files may still
refer to four phases for historical skill taxonomy. Do not let that taxonomy
overwrite the course sequence during E50 reconstruction.

## Router Implications

- Users asking for diagnosis, invisible failures, vision, or empathic funnel
  should map to `Raíz`.
- Users asking about business model, product coherence, Lean Canvas, Whole
  Product, Customer Forces, or pre-investment validation should map to
  `Semilla`.
- Users asking about PESCAR, Validation Plan, Experiment Report, campaign
  experiments, metrics, or campaign learning should map to `Brote`.
- Users asking about Meta Ads, storytelling, AAIDA, creative message, or
  flywheel should map to `Ramas`.
- Users asking about Kaizen, optimization, continuous improvement, market
  feedback loops, or sustainable growth should map to `Fruto`.

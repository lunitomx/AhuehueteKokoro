## Curriculum Placement

Module 3 is named as the germination stage. The class positions PESCAR as the
first major method for turning validated business hypotheses into market
communication, channel decisions, content, and experiments.

## Doctrine To Preserve

- PESCAR should be treated as a strategic decision process, not a generic
  content checklist.
- The fishing metaphor matters because it forces the user to ask where the
  invited person is, what context they are in, what value/bait fits, and what
  result is being sought.
- Platform choice comes after understanding segment behavior and purchase
  context.
- Content is valuable when it helps the invited person before asking for an
  acquisition decision.
- Validation plan, experiment report, ley de los sprints, and 3x3x3 are linked
  to PESCAR and are now confirmed as the next formal class in `RAW-E50-002`.

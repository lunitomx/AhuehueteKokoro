## Curriculum Placement

This class continues **Level 4: Ramas — Meta Ads y Storytelling**. It follows
the Meta Ads / Flywheel introduction and turns the flywheel into creative
assets using the Hamburguesa / AAIDA storytelling sequence.

## Doctrine To Preserve

- Meta Ads success is not primarily knowing where to click in Ads Manager.
  Correct execution depends on strategy, brand statement, segment clarity,
  customer understanding, creative structure, and sales feedback.
- A video is not just a video. It is the visible surface of strategy, model,
  customer insight, product promise, and follow-up design.
- Flywheel requires assets for each transition:
  - stranger to prospect,
  - prospect to consumer,
  - consumer to promoter.
- AAIDA / Hamburguesa sequence:
  - Attention: trigger event or real tension.
  - Affinity: mirror the customer's situation and emotion.
  - Interest: reveal the mechanism, cost, or hidden issue.
  - Desire: show the better future or relief.
  - Action: invite softly toward the next step.
- The "queso" is soft authority: proof, experience, or credibility expressed
  without ego.
- The lower bun is the human close: a soft opening, invitation, or next step
  that does not push.
- Product, marketing, and sales are a triad. A bad product/model makes
  marketing sell the wrong thing; weak marketing gives sales the wrong leads;
  weak sales feedback leaves marketing blind.
- Referrals become stronger when people know exactly who to recommend and why.

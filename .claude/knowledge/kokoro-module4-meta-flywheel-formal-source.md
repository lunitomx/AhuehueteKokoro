## Curriculum Placement

This class begins **Level 4: Ramas — Meta Ads y Storytelling**. It follows
Brote's validation discipline and applies it to paid distribution, algorithmic
learning, creative persuasion, and flywheel design.

## Doctrine To Preserve

- Meta Ads is not only campaign setup. The class should teach how to build a
  campaign that can convert inside Meta's algorithmic environment.
- Launching is fast; planning is slow. Turning on a campaign may take minutes,
  but understanding what was tested, what failed, and what should be said can
  take days.
- Trust should precede conversion. A person who has watched useful videos and
  understood the brand is a better lead than someone pushed too early to a CTA.
- Video-first creative matters because Meta can read in-platform video behavior
  more clearly than off-platform behavior blocked by privacy and tracking gaps.
- Advantage+, Conversion API, pixel visibility, placement formats, CRM/WhatsApp,
  and sales feedback are one operating system, not isolated tactics.
- The Flywheel sequence is: attract, engage/commit, delight. The commercial
  progression is: strangers, prospects, consumers, promoters.
- The worksheet activity asks the entrepreneur to identify:
  - why the ideal customer remains in the current comfort zone,
  - what message or gift could move that person,
  - what objections will appear after interest is created.

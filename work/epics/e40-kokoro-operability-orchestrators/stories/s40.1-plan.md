## Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Ignoring `.raise/` too broadly hides public typed artifacts | Ignore `.raise/*` by default but unignore `.raise/artifacts/**`. |
| Checklist becomes too technical | Use business-facing pass/fail language. |
| Secret scan flags placeholders | Report placeholders separately and verify no real values are present. |

## Heutagogical Checkpoint

### What did you learn?

A weekly operating run needs to explain coverage before insight. If only Meta Ads and GA4 are connected, the pulse should speak strongly about paid-to-site continuity but stay quiet about Google Ads or SEO.

### What would you change about the process?

Start every recurring executive workflow with the output shape first. The shape determines whether the command becomes an executive read or a metric dump.

### Are there improvements for the framework?

Yes. Weekly, Google Ads, and future acquisition runs should all share a "coverage first, interpretation second, action third" pattern.

### What are you more capable of now?

Kokoro can now support a recurring marketing leadership ritual: one weekly read that makes absence visible, interprets signals across platforms, and protects against premature action.

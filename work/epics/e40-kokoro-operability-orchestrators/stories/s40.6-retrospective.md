## Heutagogical Checkpoint

### What did you learn?

The decisive creative gate is not "do we have a prompt?" but "do we know what decision each frame moves?" Once that is clear, image generation becomes production, not strategy.

### What would you change about the process?

For creative work, design should start from the failure case. Here, the failure case was a polished visual that moved attention away from the message. That made the gate design sharper.

### Are there improvements for the framework?

Yes. Visual asset runs should all include "preserve" and "avoid" fields. They prevent the model from changing brand, model, merch, logo, or mood when the user explicitly wants continuity.

### What are you more capable of now?

Kokoro can now receive "hazme un carrusel" and respond as a creative director: promise first, story second, visual attention third, generation fourth, review before pauta.

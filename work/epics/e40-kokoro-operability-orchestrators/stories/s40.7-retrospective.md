## Heutagogical Checkpoint

### What did you learn?

The decisive launch gate is not "do we have copy?" but "do we know what would make this experiment true or false?" Without that, the launch only creates motion.

### What would you change about the process?

Start launch orchestration from invalidation thresholds earlier. They make the recommendation more executive because they define when to stop, revise, or scale.

### Are there improvements for the framework?

Yes. Kokoro orchestration runs should separate "prepare", "launch", and "blocked" states. That gives a business leader a usable next step even when the request is not ready.

### What are you more capable of now?

Kokoro can now receive "quiero lanzar esta creacion" and respond like a strategic launch operator: validate readiness, choose the correct tactical skills, define the experiment, protect tracking, and set the first readback before creating assets.

# S40.8 Scope: Acquisition Run

## In Scope

- Create `.claude/commands/kokoro-acquisition-run.md`.
- Orchestrate existing capabilities:
  - `/kokoro-factory`
  - `/kokoro-funnel`
  - `/kokoro-mafia`
  - `/kokoro-landing`
  - `/kokoro-analytics`
  - `/kokoro-scorecard`
  - `/kokoro-tracking-check`
  - `/kokoro-google-ads-run`
  - `/kokoro-weekly-marketing-run`
- Apply `kokoro-orchestrator-contract.md`.
- Define acquisition readiness and diagnostic gates:
  - invitado/context resolved
  - acquisition objective clear
  - current channel mix known or explicitly unknown
  - platform/MCP health checked when data-backed analysis is requested
  - landing and CTA path known
  - tracking and CRM/follow-up visibility known
  - unit economics or proxy metrics identified
  - offer clarity and friction assessed
- Define fallback behavior for missing platform data, missing tracking, missing landing, or missing sales/follow-up context.
- Update executive router to mark `/kokoro-acquisition-run` available.

## Out of Scope

- Creating real campaigns.
- Editing real landing pages.
- Changing tracking or CRM configuration.
- Connecting real platform accounts.
- Saving private acquisition reports in the public repo.
- Building a sales CRM workflow.
- Rewriting the underlying tactical skills.

## Done Criteria

- `/kokoro-acquisition-run` exists as a command contract.
- The command includes an acquisition readiness table and bottleneck map.
- The command includes output sections for evidence, assumptions, bottleneck diagnosis, offer/landing/tracking review, what not to touch yet, next actions, and permission gate.
- Router marks `/kokoro-acquisition-run` as available.
- Verification confirms no credentials, private paths, real guest data, private reports, or mutation assumptions were added.

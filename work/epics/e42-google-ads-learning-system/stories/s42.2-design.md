# S42.2 Design

## Current Surface
- `kokoro-close` writes `summary`, `hallazgos`, `artifacts`, and `next_action`.
- `kokoro-gads` also writes session history when a guest is resolved.

## Target Behavior
- Persist the learning record with optional Google Ads context.
- Keep the record aligned with `kokoro-learning-dashboard.md`.
- Ensure open/close vocabulary matches the schema.

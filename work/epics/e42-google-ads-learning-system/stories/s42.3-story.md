# S42.3 Story: Surface Learning on Open

## Story
Update the open flow so returning invitado work starts from the recent learning
history, not from a blank recap.

## Outcome
`/kokoro-open` becomes the control surface for understanding what happened last
time and what should happen next.

## Acceptance
- Open shows the last relevant Google Ads sessions.
- Open surfaces `next_action` as the proposed focus.
- Open can show `learning_state` when present.
- The output stays brief enough to be read quickly.

## Dependencies
- S42.1
- S42.2
- `.claude/commands/kokoro-open.md`
